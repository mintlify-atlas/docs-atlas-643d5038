The web version of City Report serves as the operational hub for municipal staff to centralize, manage, and track urban reports from citizens. This platform provides authorized personnel with the tools needed to organize, classify, and respond to citizen concerns efficiently.

## Who uses the web platform

The web platform is designed for municipal staff members aged 22 to 60 years, including:

- Administrative personnel who process and categorize reports
- Operations staff who coordinate responses to citizen concerns
- Supervisors who monitor report status and team performance
- Decision-makers who analyze data to allocate resources effectively

## How it improves municipal operations

### Structured information flow

The web platform standardizes how citizen reports enter and move through the municipal system. Instead of informal channels that lead to lost information, you work with a formal, reliable digital structure that captures every detail.

### Better organization and control

You can classify reports according to predefined criteria, set priorities based on urgency, and assign them to the right teams. This organizational capability means fewer reports fall through the cracks and more efficient use of your team's time.

<Note>
The web platform eliminates unnecessary intermediaries between citizens and municipal services, creating a direct channel for communication.
</Note>

### Data-driven decision making

With all reports centralized in one system, you gain visibility into patterns and trends. This data helps you:

- Identify recurring urban issues that need systematic solutions
- Allocate resources to areas with the highest need
- Measure response times and team performance
- Demonstrate accountability through transparent tracking

## Workflow overview

Your typical workflow on the web platform follows this pattern:

1. **Receive** - New citizen reports appear in your dashboard as they're submitted
2. **Review** - You examine report details including description, location, photos, and priority
3. **Classify** - You categorize the report and assign it to the appropriate department or team member
4. **Track** - You monitor progress as your team works on resolving the issue
5. **Update** - You change report status as work progresses, keeping citizens informed
6. **Close** - You mark reports as resolved once the issue is addressed

<Info>
Every status change you make on the web platform is visible to citizens through the mobile app, promoting transparency and trust.
</Info>

## Integration with mobile platform

The web version works in tandem with the mobile app that citizens use. When you update a report's status on the web platform, citizens see those changes in real-time on their mobile devices. This integration creates a complete communication loop:

- Citizens submit and track reports through mobile
- You manage and resolve reports through web
- Both sides stay informed throughout the process

This complementary relationship strengthens the connection between your municipality and the community you serve.
