The mobile version of City Report provides citizens with direct access to consult and track their urban reports. This app promotes transparency, institutional trust, and social participation by giving citizens clear visibility into how the municipality handles their concerns.

## Who uses the mobile app

The mobile app is designed for citizens aged 18 to 65 years who:

- Have basic digital interaction skills and use mobile devices regularly
- Want to participate actively in reporting urban issues
- Need to track the status of their submitted reports
- Expect transparent communication from their municipal government

<Info>
This age range includes the adult population most likely to engage with digital civic services and participate in community improvement efforts.
</Info>

## How it enhances citizen experience

### Quick and accessible information

You no longer need to call municipal offices or visit in person to check on your reports. The mobile app gives you instant access to your report history and current status, saving you time and reducing frustration.

### Transparency and trust

The app eliminates uncertainty about what happens after you submit a report. You see:

- When your report was received
- Who is handling it
- What stage of resolution it's in
- When it was completed

This visibility builds trust in municipal services and demonstrates institutional commitment to addressing your concerns.

<Note>
Every status change made by municipal staff on the web platform appears immediately in your mobile app, ensuring you're always informed.
</Note>

### Reduced uncertainty

Instead of wondering whether your concern was heard or forgotten, you have concrete evidence of municipal response. The structured format and clear status indicators give you confidence that your report is being handled appropriately.

## User journey

Your experience with the mobile app typically follows this flow:

1. **Access** - You open the app and log in to your account
2. **View reports** - You see a list of all reports you've submitted
3. **Select report** - You tap on a specific report to view full details
4. **Check status** - You review the current status and any updates from municipal staff
5. **Monitor progress** - You return periodically to see how the situation evolves
6. **Verify resolution** - You confirm when the issue has been resolved

### Understanding report status

The app uses standardized status indicators that help you understand exactly where your report stands:

- **Submitted** - Your report has been received and is waiting for review
- **In review** - Municipal staff are examining your report and assigning it
- **Assigned** - A specific team is now responsible for addressing your concern
- **In progress** - Work is actively being done to resolve the issue
- **Resolved** - The issue has been addressed and the report is closed

<Info>
Clear status definitions reduce confusion and eliminate the need for follow-up calls to municipal offices.
</Info>

## Promoting civic participation

The mobile app does more than just provide information—it strengthens your relationship with your municipality:

### Direct communication channel

You have a formal, structured way to communicate urban issues directly to the people who can address them. No intermediaries, no lost messages.

### Evidence of engagement

The app serves as a record of your civic participation. You can see the history of issues you've reported and the municipality's responses, demonstrating mutual engagement.

### Encouraging involvement

When you see that your reports lead to real action, you're more likely to continue participating in community improvement efforts. The app's transparency creates a positive feedback loop that encourages ongoing civic engagement.

## Integration with web platform

The mobile app is the citizen-facing side of a complete system. While you use the mobile app to track reports, municipal staff use the web platform to manage them. This integration ensures:

- Information stays synchronized between both platforms
- Status updates flow seamlessly from staff to citizens
- Communication happens in real-time without delays
- Both sides of the relationship have the tools they need

Together, the mobile and web platforms create a modern, accessible, and controlled digital model for citizen services.
