City Report establishes a structured system for tracking the status of urban reports from submission through resolution. You can monitor progress in real-time, whether you're a citizen checking your report or municipal staff overseeing operations.

## Status visibility

The system provides clear, standardized status indicators that eliminate uncertainty about where reports stand in the resolution process.

## For citizens

The mobile platform gives you easy access to track your reports and stay informed about municipal responses.

### Quick consultations

You can check the status of your reports anytime from your mobile device. The interface is designed to be clear and accessible, providing information in a format that's easy to understand.

### Transparency in action

Instead of wondering whether your report was received or is being addressed, you have concrete visibility into its progress. This transparency builds trust in municipal services.

### Reduced uncertainty

You no longer need to call city offices or visit in person to ask about your report. The system provides the information you need directly, saving you time and effort.

<Note>
  The mobile platform is optimized for citizens aged 18-65 with basic digital literacy and smartphone access.
</Note>

## For municipal staff

The web platform gives you comprehensive oversight of all reports and their current status across your organization.

### Operational monitoring

You can see at a glance which reports are pending, in progress, or resolved. This visibility helps you manage workloads and ensure nothing falls through the cracks.

### Performance tracking

Monitor how quickly reports move through different status stages. You can identify bottlenecks in your processes and measure your team's responsiveness.

### Accountability

Clear status tracking creates accountability within your organization. You can see who is responsible for each report and ensure work is progressing as expected.

<Info>
  Status updates made in the web platform are immediately visible to citizens in the mobile app, creating a seamless flow of information.
</Info>

## Standard status workflow

While specific implementations may vary, City Report typically structures the report lifecycle through clear stages:

1. **Submitted** - The report has been received and entered into the system
2. **Under review** - Staff are evaluating the report and determining appropriate action
3. **In progress** - Work is actively being done to address the reported issue
4. **Resolved** - The issue has been addressed and the report is complete

Each status transition is logged with timestamp and responsible party information for complete traceability.

## Benefits of structured tracking

### Improved communication

Status tracking eliminates information gaps between citizens and municipal government. Both parties can see the same information and have shared understanding of progress.

### Faster follow-up

When everyone can see current status, follow-up is more efficient. Citizens know when to expect updates, and staff can focus on resolution rather than answering status inquiries.

### Trust building

Consistent visibility into report status demonstrates municipal commitment to accountability and responsive service. You build citizen confidence by showing that reports are actively managed.

## Integration with reporting channels

The status tracking system works across both platforms:

- **Web platform**: Municipal staff update status as they take action on reports
- **Mobile platform**: Citizens see these updates reflected immediately in their report view

This integration creates a direct, formal communication channel that reduces reliance on informal methods and ensures information consistency.

<Note>
  The status tracking system is designed to reduce the need for citizens to contact municipal offices directly, freeing up staff time for actual resolution work.
</Note>

## Impact on service delivery

By providing clear status visibility, City Report strengthens the relationship between municipalities and citizens. You create a more professional service experience that demonstrates respect for citizen concerns and commitment to responsive governance.
