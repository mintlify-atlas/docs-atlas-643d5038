## What is City Report?

City Report is a comprehensive digital platform designed to strengthen communication between municipalities and their citizens. The system enables efficient management of urban issue reports through two complementary platforms: a web application for municipal staff and a mobile application for citizens.

By providing a structured, transparent channel for reporting and tracking urban issues, City Report helps municipalities deliver better public services while building trust with their communities.

## Who is City Report for?

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Citizens" icon="users">
    Residents aged 18-65 who want to report urban issues and track their resolution. The mobile app makes it easy to submit reports and monitor progress transparently.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Municipal staff" icon="building">

## Why City Report?

Traditional methods of reporting urban issues often rely on informal channels that lead to lost information, lack of follow-up, and diminished public trust. City Report addresses these challenges by:

- **Centralizing communication** - Establishing a direct, formal channel between citizens and municipal authorities
- **Ensuring transparency** - Providing clear status updates and traceability for every report
- **Improving efficiency** - Streamlining information flows so municipal teams can prioritize and respond faster
- **Building trust** - Demonstrating institutional commitment to addressing community needs

<Note>
  City Report eliminates unnecessary intermediaries and standardizes the reporting process, making it easier for both citizens and municipal staff to communicate effectively.
</Note>

## Key benefits

### For citizens

- Submit urban issue reports quickly from your mobile device
- Track the status of your reports in real-time
- Access clear, transparent information about resolution progress
- Participate actively in improving your community

### For municipal staff

- Organize and classify incoming reports systematically
- Monitor operational workflows and track resolution progress
- Prioritize issues based on urgency and resources
- Make data-driven decisions to improve public services

## How it works

City Report creates a seamless flow of information:

1. **Citizens submit reports** through the mobile app, documenting urban issues with photos and descriptions
2. **Municipal staff receive and classify** reports through the web platform's centralized system
3. **Teams assign and track** resolution progress with defined statuses and workflows
4. **Citizens monitor updates** and receive notifications about their reports
5. **Municipalities analyze data** to identify patterns and improve resource allocation

<Info>
  The web and mobile platforms work together to create a modern, accessible, and controlled model for digital civic engagement.
</Info>
